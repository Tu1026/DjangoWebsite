Follow these simple steps to get started.

Get your discord name

You will need you discord name in the format of "name#1234" when registering for an account. You can simply do this by going over to discord and click on your own username in the bottom left corner and your username will be copied automatically. In the example, my discord_id would be Pandaspy#8354
